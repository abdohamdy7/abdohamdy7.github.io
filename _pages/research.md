My research is centered on autonomous driving and intelligent transportation systems, with emphasis on decision-making under uncertainty and deployable autonomy stacks.

## Research Themes

### Risk-aware Motion Planning and Control

I study online risk-bounded motion planning for autonomous driving in mixed urban traffic. This work combines probabilistic planning, combinatorial optimization, mixed-integer programming, competitive online algorithms, and regret-aware decision-making.

### Intelligent Transportation Systems

I develop optimization and learning-based methods for traffic-signal control, eco-driving, and connected emergency-vehicle prioritization. The goal is to improve safety, energy efficiency, and network-level performance as autonomy penetration increases.

### Learning-based Control and Interaction-aware Autonomy

My work also includes reinforcement learning and interaction-aware optimal control for challenging systems such as autonomous vehicles, race cars, and active suspension platforms, often in combination with classical control techniques including LQR, LQG, PID, MPC, and full-state feedback.

### Trajectory Prediction and Data-driven Scene Understanding

I contribute to trajectory-prediction research and dataset-building pipelines for autonomous driving, including multi-task benchmarks and data collection, anonymization, and perception workflows.
