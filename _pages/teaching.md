## Teaching Approach

- Bridge theory and implementation through simulation, coding assignments, and project-based learning.
- Use research-driven examples from robotics, autonomous driving, and intelligent transportation systems.
- Emphasize algorithmic clarity, reproducibility, and engineering discipline in student work.
