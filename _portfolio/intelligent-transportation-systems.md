This project thread studies how connected and autonomous vehicles can interact with transportation infrastructure to improve safety, efficiency, and emergency response. The work spans signal-aware planning, eco-driving, and control strategies for connected emergency vehicles.

## Core Questions

- How can autonomous vehicles exploit traffic-signal information to reduce delay and energy use?
- How can emergency vehicles be prioritized without causing unacceptable disruption to the rest of the network?
- How can intelligent control strategies remain effective in mixed traffic with both connected and non-connected road users?

## Methods

- Traffic-signal-aware planning and control
- Dynamic path planning and signal preemption
- Microscopic traffic simulation and policy evaluation
- Learning-based and optimization-based control strategies
