This project thread focuses on local planning and decision-making methods for autonomous driving under uncertainty. The core objective is to produce motion plans that remain computationally tractable while respecting explicit risk constraints in dynamic traffic environments.

## Core Questions

- How can local planners remain safe under uncertain predictions of surrounding traffic agents?
- How can spatiotemporal risk be represented in a way that supports fast online optimization?
- What theoretical guarantees can be provided for practical real-time planning systems?

## Methods

- Graph-based and lattice-based local planning
- Constrained shortest-path formulations
- Competitive and regret-aware online optimization
- Risk-bounded decision-making under prediction uncertainty
