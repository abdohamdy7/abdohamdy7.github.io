This project thread covers data-driven perception research for autonomous driving, with emphasis on trajectory prediction, benchmark development, and research infrastructure for datasets. It includes survey work, multi-task benchmark design, and privacy-aware tooling for research data release.

## Core Questions

- What limitations prevent current trajectory-prediction methods from generalizing to real traffic conditions?
- How can benchmark datasets better support multi-task autonomous-driving research?
- How can sensitive driving data be shared responsibly while preserving research value?

## Methods

- Survey and taxonomy building for trajectory prediction
- Benchmark dataset design and curation
- Privacy-aware anonymization pipelines
- Data-driven evaluation for perception and scene understanding

- Dataset preparation and annotation workflows
- Benchmark design for multi-task learning
