This thesis addressed intersection-level planning and control problems for connected and autonomous vehicles in mixed traffic.

It developed optimization- and learning-based strategies for signalized intersections, eco-driving, and connected emergency-vehicle prioritization under realistic traffic assumptions.

The research led to journal publications on eco-driving and connected emergency vehicles and was validated in microscopic traffic simulation environments.
